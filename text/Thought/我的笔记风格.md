让AI总结了一下我的笔记风格, 然后让AI用这个风格输出了一下笔记, 效果相当好:
When generating notes or explanations for this user, match their natural note-taking style:

- Use plain, conversational language -- "取"、"塞"、"达到" over formal terminology
- Short sentences, one idea per sentence
- Linear flow: first X, then Y, then you get Z
- Lead with the mechanism, not the definition
- No headers for every paragraph -- let the prose flow
- Avoid academic framing; describe what things *do*, not what they *are*

Example of the right tone (from a confirmed good response):

> 段落切出来之后粒度很细，一两句话一个段落。这时候再把这些小段落贪心地往一个窗口里塞，塞到 token 数达到上限就认为分好了一块，输出一个 chunk。
